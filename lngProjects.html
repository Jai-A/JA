LNG PROJECTS, CURTIS ISLAND  

Area: 100ha
Client: Walz Construction Co, MacMahon, See Civil, Bechtel, Thiess
Services: Revegetation & Consultation

Lanyonscape provided expert consultation and revegetation services for a range of jobs on Bechtel’s $90 Million LNG export facility construction sites on Curtis Island. Lanyonscapes secured several continuing contracts, as well as many “one off” jobs for a range of sub contractors.

Services included:

  • Consultation & application of soil treatments and seeding in several stages to meet legislative requirements
  • Temporary irrigation
  • Adherence to strict time frames for delivery
  • All safety criteria met

Lanyonscapes has also provided site maintenance, supply and services direct to Bechtel.
