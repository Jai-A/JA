In early 2015, Lanyonscapes was contracted to vegetate a sheer batter face, north-west of Gladstone, QLD. Due to the challenging nature of the site and a lack of existing topsoil, Hydro Compost Organics Blanket (HCOB) was proposed for use as it would provide a growing medium (compost) for the grass while also preventing further erosion of the area.

A trial area was set aside to determine if HCOB would be an effective treatment option.

The site was irrigated to aid grass growth.

The site was sprayed in January 2015.

Progress photos were taken to document growth.

By May, 2015, our client was satisfied with the results and opted to continue treatment across the entire batter.
