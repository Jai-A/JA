Flexterra® is an alternative to hydromulch for high risk sites or areas where water availability is limited.It’s applied in a similar manner to hydromulch but instead, cane mulch is substituted for the Flexterra fibre.

We used Flexterra on a site near Gladstone in December 2015. The site typically has poor soil conditions and no resources available for irrigation.

These photos were taken in January 2016. A good rainfall over Christmas really helped get things get started!
