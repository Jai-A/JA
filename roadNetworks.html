ROAD NETWORKS  

Lanyonscapes provides a complete service package from planting media management plans to establishment and monitoring, including minor civil works for ground preparation and topsoil amelioration.

We also offer a cost-effective alternative for topsoil that meets MRTS16 specifications. The Hydro Compost Organics Blanket (HCOB) has been developed by Lanyonscapes to deliver outstanding results in tough Australian conditions.

Lanyonscapes key road projects include:

  • Sandy Corner (Decmil)
  • WICET (AGJV)
  • Dysart Road Relocation (York Civil)
  • 8 Mile Creek (Georgiou)
