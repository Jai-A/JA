RESEARCH & INNOVATION  

Lanyonscapes works to actively improve:

  • product effectiveness and suitability
  • application efficiency
  • long term outcomes for clients.

Our Research and Development Team are continually developing new products and innovative applications. Recent developments include:

  • A  spray-on organics blanket system – the Hydro Compost Organics Blanket (HCOB) is used in revegetation situations to replace topsoil, control sediment and prevent erosion
  • Hotpods® – an innovative alternative for mine site spoil rehabilitation which include seed, fertiliser, soil ameliorants and growing media pressed into a pellet type ball.
