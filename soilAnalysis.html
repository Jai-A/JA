Soil Analysis  

In order to ensure the best possible results, we recommend our clients have their siteâ€™s soil analysed prior to commencing revegetation works. This is especially valuable for sites with imported soil or heavily disturbed sites, such as mine sites, or where soil has been left bare for extensive periods of time.

Lanyonscapes provides services for:

  • Soil sampling
  • Analysis
  • Recommendations

Our qualified staff conduct soil sampling (or can assist your staff in taking samples) and send these samples to a NATA-accredited lab for analysis. With these results, we consult with our fully-qualified Soil Scientist to develop a Soil Report detailing amelioration agents or other products that could be used to create optimal growing conditions within the soil.

These results can also be used to tailor plant or seed recommendations for your site.
