GROUND PREPARATION  

Appropriate ground preparation is crucial to successful revegetation projects and Lanyonscapes has the experience and soil science expertise to ensure your project achieves a successful, cost-effective outcome, without the need for costly “re-dos”.

Our ground preparation services include:

  • Ripping
  • Roughening
  • Tyning
  • Load, haul and place topsoil
  • Load, haul and place site mulch
  • Soil amelioration.
