MINE SITE REHABILITATION  

Lanyonscapes has decades of experience in mine site revegetation.

We offer a complete service from ground preparation and planting to management and monitoring, including minor civil works.

Our products include a new, innovative seeding method using Hotpods®. This exclusive product is a reliable and cost effective alternative that can be tailored to your project.

We provide broad acre rehabilitation and revegetation via:

  • mechanical seeding
  • aerial seeding
  • hotpods®
  • sourcing and planting of tubestock
  • installation of temporary or permanent irrigation
  • monitoring, data collection, collating & report writing
  • maintenance and weed control
  • topsoiling
  • mulching.
