In November 2015, Lanyonscapes completed rehabilitation works of an evaporation dam near Collinsville, QLD.Approximately 4.5ha was hydromulched using a pasture seed mix, supplied by the client.

Our client recently shared their positive results with us.

Despite a drier wet season than usual, the site achieved the desired level of grass cover. Feedback from cattle was also positive, finding the grass irresistible!
