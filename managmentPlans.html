MANAGEMENT PLANS  

Planting Media Management Plans

Planting Media Management Plans document the inspections, checks and protocols Lanyonscapes follow before applying hydromulch or hydroseed. This plan ensures your project’s plant media is of the correct quality and adequately prepared.

The document explains the identification and decision-making process to assess the soil and specifies how the soil is to be treated and prepared prior to hydromulch and hydroseed application.

It’s recommended that soil testing be conducted prior to the preparation of the plan to ensure optimal results.

Vegetation Assessment & Monitoring Reports
