Fishermans Landing Bunds  

Area: 20ha
Client: Van Oord Dredging International JV
Services: Consultation

Reclamation bunds from massive dredging works of Gladstone Harbour present a difficult task of revegetating 20m high sandy, salty batters.

Lanyonscapes carried out vegetation trials and developed a tailored recommendation which resulted in a positive outcome.
